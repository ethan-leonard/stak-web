Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use stak.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Email address and profile information
  • Workout data and fitness metrics
  • Device information and usage patterns
  • Performance data and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your fitness data
  • Send important updates
  • Provide customer support

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only for:

  • Service providers that help operate our app
  • Legal requirements or valid legal requests
  • Safety and security of our users

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, though no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

stak is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will notify you of changes.
